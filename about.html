About Me

Just a little view into my family.

My Childhood

I was born August 31, 2001 in Salt Lake City, Utah. I was born to two wonderful parents, Jon and Leslie Rogers.

In total, I have 3 siblings. Ian is my eldest brother he is currently 26 years old. I have an older sister, her name is Xana and she is 23 years old. Lastly, I have a younger brother who is 19 years old named Christian.

Hobbies

Growing up I loved dancing. I have loved ballet for as long as I can remember. Wearing a tutu helped me fulfill my dreams of becoming a princess.

My parents also forced me into swimming in my childhood. At first, I completely hated the exercise and wanted to quit as soon as I could. However, overtime I fell in love with the sport and the way it made me feel. I ended up contuining the sport all throughout high school becoming Captian of my swim team. I will never forget the feeling of going to state and winning a medal at regionals.

If you know me, you know I always have a song stuck in my mind. That's why I have always loved singing and choir. From 7th grade all the way to graduating High School, I was in a choir.

My Mission

I got my mission call during the beginning of Covid 2020. I was called to serve in the California San Diego Mission Spanish Speaking. I returned in April of 2022. My life will forever be changed through my service in California. I truly learned what it means to be a disciple of Christ, to humble myself and immerse myself in his love.
