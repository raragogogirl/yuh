Will it Airfry Wednesday

An Airfry Recipe and a Wholesome Message

Started Instagram and Facebook Show October, 2021

I started Will it Airfry Wednesday in October 2022. I was serving as a missionary for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in San Diego, California. I wanted to spread the gospel in a fun, creative way. I use my Airfry almost everyday and I wanted others to experience the joy of recipe making and scripture reading with me.

Every episode I try to airfry something new. This could be anything from ice cream to jello! I never know how the food will turn out, it is always a suprise. While the food is cooking, I take the time to share one of my favorite scripture verses. I share a brief testimony on how that verse has impacted my life.

After the food is done cooking, it is time for the taste test. Then me and my companion will give a rating on how the food tasted out of 10.

I always looked forward to creating another episode weekly.

The Miracle of the Show

At the beginning of the creation of the show, most of the views were from members or other missionaries. However, over time more and more strangers I didn't know kept finding the show.

In January 2022, a man named Jordan messaged me after seeing one of my episodes. He was so intrigued with the message I shared and wanted to learn more.

Romans 8:39. Nor height, nor depth, nor any other creature, shall be able to separate us from the love of God, which is in Christ Jesus our Lord.

I am pleased to say that after meeting with us multiple times a week, in the beginning of March 2022 Jordan was baptized as a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

The Reboot

After 2 seasons, I finished my last episode of Will it Airfry Wednesday in April 2022. I will forever have so much love in my heart for the series that I created. I decided to pass on the legacy to my great friend Elder Rhodes. I gifted him my Airfryer and he is continuing the legacy to this day.

Elder Rhodes on the right-hand side.

I am grateful for all that the show has given me and those closest to me. I would encourage you to take the time to connect with God, either by reading the scriptures or through watching this show.
